About This Tour

Experience the Red Sea like never before aboard the Royal Seascope semi-submarine. This unique journey allows you to explore the underwater world without getting wet. From the marina, you will sail on a comfortable vessel equipped with a special underwater viewing deck, where you can admire vibrant coral reefs and colorful marine life through large panoramic windows.

Tour Program

  • Marina Departure: Start your journey from the marina aboard the Royal Seascope vessel.
  • Sailing Experience: Enjoy a relaxing short cruise over the Red Sea waters.
  • Underwater Deck: Descend to the glass viewing cabin and observe marine life in its natural habitat.
  • Marine Exploration: Watch colorful fish and coral reefs while listening to the live marine guide explanations.
  • Photography Time: Capture amazing underwater scenes with your camera without getting wet.
  • Return to Marina: End your unforgettable semi-submarine adventure back at the harbor.

Tour Highlights

  • Unique semi-submarine underwater viewing experience
  • Safe and comfortable for all ages and non-swimmers
  • Discover coral reefs and tropical marine life
  • Professional marine guide commentary during the trip
