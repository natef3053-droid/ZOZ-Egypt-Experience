About This Tour

Embark on a full-day spiritual journey across Egypt’s Eastern Desert in a comfortable air-conditioned vehicle with refreshing drinks provided throughout the trip. Travel through the desert landscapes toward some of the world’s oldest Christian monasteries, where history, faith, and silence meet in a deeply spiritual atmosphere.

Tour Program

  • Hotel Transfer: Morning pick-up in a comfortable air-conditioned vehicle with refreshments on board.
  • Desert Journey: Travel across the Eastern Desert toward the ancient monastic routes.
  • St. Anthony Monastery: Visit the oldest monastery in the world and explore its historic churches and sacred library with expert guides.
  • Spiritual Exploration: Discover ancient caves, monastic cells, and places where Saint Anthony once lived in complete solitude.
  • Lunch Experience: Enjoy a traditional Eastern lunch in a local oasis with vegetarian and fasting-friendly options available.
  • St. Paul Monastery: Visit the fortress-like monastery of St. Paul the Hermit and explore its sacred desert surroundings.
  • Return Journey: Head back while enjoying the desert sunset and peaceful landscapes.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the oldest Christian monasteries in the world
  • Explore St. Anthony & St. Paul monasteries
  • Discover ancient caves and sacred monastic history
  • Authentic desert journey with expert historical guidance
