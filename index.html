Frequently Asked Questions

Common queries to help you prepare for your upcoming Egyptian adventure.

What is the best time to visit Egypt?

The best time to visit is between October and April, when the weather is mild and perfect for exploring outdoor historical sites and enjoying the Red Sea.

Do I need a visa to visit Egypt?

Entry requirements depend on your nationality. Many nationalities can obtain a visa on arrival at the airport, while others may need to apply online in advance. We recommend checking with your local embassy.

How can I book a tour with ZOZ?

Booking is easy! You can reach out directly via our WhatsApp link on our website, or send us an email. We will guide you through the selection and confirmation process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including cash (Euros, Dollars, or Egyptian Pounds) and bank transfers. We will clarify the details during your booking confirmation.

Can I request a private or customized tour?

Absolutely! We specialize in crafting bespoke journeys. Whether you want a private family tour or a specific itinerary, we are here to bring your vision to life.
