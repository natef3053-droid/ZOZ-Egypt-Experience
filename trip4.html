About This Tour

Experience the wonders of ancient Egypt on a full-day trip from Hurghada to Luxor. Travel comfortably through the desert and explore some of the most iconic historical landmarks in the world. From grand temples to royal tombs, this journey offers a deep dive into the rich history and culture of Egypt.

Tour Program

  • Departure: Early pick-up from your hotel in Hurghada, followed by a short gathering period and security check before departure.
  • Rest Stop: A relaxing break at kilometer 85 after approximately 1.5 hours of travel.
  • Karnak Temple: Arrival in Luxor and visit to the magnificent Karnak Temple complex.
  • Lunch: Enjoy a delicious meal at a local restaurant in Luxor.
  • Colossi of Memnon: Stop by the impressive statues of Amenhotep III.
  • Valley of the Kings: Explore the ancient royal tombs of Egypt’s greatest pharaohs.
  • Hatshepsut Temple: Visit the stunning temple of Queen Hatshepsut, one of Egypt’s most powerful rulers.
  • Return: Journey back to Hurghada after an unforgettable historical adventure.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the world-famous Karnak Temple complex
  • Discover the secrets of the Valley of the Kings
  • Explore the Temple of Queen Hatshepsut
  • Comfortable transport with guided experience
