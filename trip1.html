About This Tour

Discover the wonders of the Red Sea on our exclusive full-day boat trip. Departing from Hurghada Marina, this journey takes you to the finest snorkeling spots to witness vibrant coral reefs and exotic marine life. Enjoy a relaxing day on pristine island beaches, complemented by a delicious lunch and an atmosphere of total tranquility.

Tour Program

  • Hotel Transfer: Comfortable morning pick-up from your hotel to Hurghada Marina.
  • Sailing & Snorkeling: Board the vessel and head to open-water spots for world-class snorkeling.
  • Coral Reef Exploration: Discover hidden underwater treasures and colorful marine ecosystems.
  • Island Visit: Time to relax on golden sands, enjoy a fresh island banquet, and soak in the sun.
  • Return Journey: Sail back to the marina as the sun begins to set, concluding your sea adventure.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit to top-rated Red Sea snorkeling locations
  • Exclusive island experience with golden beaches
  • Freshly prepared lunch served on board/island
  • Small group atmosphere for personalized service
