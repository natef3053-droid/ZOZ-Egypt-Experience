About This Tour

Embark on an unforgettable 3-day safari adventure through Egypt’s most breathtaking deserts. Starting from your location (Hurghada, Cairo, Sharm El Sheikh, or elsewhere), journey to Bahariya Oasis and explore a world of natural wonders, unique landscapes, and authentic Bedouin experiences. This tour blends adventure, relaxation, and rare photography opportunities in one extraordinary trip.

Tour Program

  • Day 1 – Bahariya Oasis Exploration: Early departure to Bahariya Oasis. Visit natural sulfur springs known for their healing benefits, then head to the English Mountain for a stunning panoramic view. Continue to Dest Mountain and enjoy walking and photography at the sand dunes before relaxing overnight in a traditional Bedouin hotel.
  • Day 2 – Black Desert & White Desert: After breakfast, depart at 9 AM to the Black Desert and explore the volcanic Marsos Mountain. Visit an authentic Bedouin village for lunch, then continue to Crystal Mountain and Agabat Valley for sandboarding and photos. Head to the New White Desert to witness incredible rock formations shaped like mushrooms, animals, and more. Enjoy a magical desert camp night with Bedouin dinner, tea, music, and stargazing.
  • Day 3 – Sunrise & Return: Capture the sunrise in the White Desert, followed by breakfast. Visit the Old White Desert and revisit Mushroom Mountain from a new perspective for unique photos. Return to Bahariya Oasis for a short rest, then travel back to your original location.

Tour Highlights

  • Full desert safari across White & Black Desert landscapes
  • Unique Bedouin accommodation and cultural experience
  • Visit to Crystal Mountain and Agabat Valley
  • Sandboarding and panoramic desert photography
  • Magical camping night under stars with traditional dinner
