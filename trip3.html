About This Tour

Enjoy a complete desert safari experience filled with adventure, culture, and unforgettable moments. Explore the vast desert landscapes with thrilling rides and immerse yourself in authentic Bedouin life, ending your day with a magical evening under the stars.

Tour Program

  • Buggy Ride: Experience the excitement of driving a beach buggy through the desert dunes.
  • Jeep Safari: Enjoy an adventurous ride across the desert in a 4x4 jeep.
  • Camel Ride & Bedouin Village: Discover traditional life with a camel ride and visit to a Bedouin village.
  • Evening Show: Watch vibrant folkloric performances in a unique desert atmosphere.
  • Dinner Under the Stars: Savor a delicious dinner in a magical setting illuminated by the moonlight.

Tour Highlights

  • Thrilling buggy driving experience in the desert
  • Authentic Bedouin culture and camel riding
  • Live entertainment and folkloric shows
  • Romantic dinner in a peaceful desert atmosphere
