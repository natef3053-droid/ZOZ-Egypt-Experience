About This Tour

Experience the magic of Cairo on a full-day journey from Hurghada. Travel across the desert to Egypt’s vibrant capital and explore its most iconic landmarks, including the Egyptian Museum and the legendary Pyramids of Giza. This tour combines history, culture, and unforgettable moments in one perfectly organized day.

Tour Program

  • Hotel Transfer: Early morning pick-up from your hotel in Hurghada with a comfortable air-conditioned vehicle.
  • Travel to Cairo: Scenic drive with a short rest stop along the way and security check procedures.
  • Egyptian Museum Visit: Discover ancient treasures, royal artifacts, and the rich history of ancient Egypt.
  • Lunch Break: Enjoy a delicious meal at a local restaurant in Cairo.
  • Pyramids of Giza: Explore the Great Pyramids and take unforgettable photos, with optional camel rides available.

Tour Highlights

  • Visit the world-famous Egyptian Museum
  • Explore the iconic Pyramids of Giza
  • Optional camel riding experience at the pyramids
  • Available VIP and private tour options
