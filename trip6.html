About This Tour

Enjoy a unique horse riding experience combined with the beauty of the sea. This tour takes you to a professional horse stable where you will get introduced to well-trained horses and receive full safety instructions from experienced trainers. Whether you are a beginner or have previous experience, this journey is designed to be safe, relaxing, and unforgettable.

Tour Program

  • Stable Visit: Arrival at the horse stable and introduction to the horses and environment.
  • Safety Briefing: Professional trainers provide essential riding and safety instructions.
  • Horse Riding Experience: Start your journey riding through open areas at your own pace.
  • Sea Adventure: Ride your horse into the sea and enjoy a truly unique and refreshing experience.
  • Return to Stable: Head back safely to the stable after completing your ride.

Tour Highlights

  • Professional and beginner-friendly horse riding experience
  • Ride horses into the sea for a unique adventure
  • Trained horses and expert supervision for maximum safety
  • Flexible duration (2 or 3 hours based on your preference)
